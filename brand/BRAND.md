# Glow social brand kit

Dark luxe theme matching the product. Audience: self-employed lash, nail and brow techs.

## One-line positioning

**Glow is the booking system built for self-employed beauty techs — your page, your clients, zero commission.**

## Voice

- Plain English. No SaaS jargon.
- Confident and established, not “we’re launching soon”.
- Speak tech-to-tech: no-shows, deposits, Fresha, bio link, infills, patch tests.

## Colours

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk, semibold, tight tracking
- **Body:** Inter, regular/medium
- **On-image minimum:** 48px headline (1080 templates), 28px subcopy
